What I do

Design

Web Design - that's what it says on the tin - that's what you'll get.

No nonsense design

No bells and whistles (unless your website is selling bells & whistles) - I am a firm believer in design that includes only what is necessary.

User experience (UX)

Put the user experience first - if your customer has a bad experience on your website they are much less likely to do business with you again. I specialize in producing sites which put the user experience first - this starts with organizing your content and ends with making sure that the right content is presented to the user in the right place.

Evidence-based design

Wherever possible I use research & evidence to inform designs.

Build

I can build you a website which is easy to update, mobile friendly and it won't cost you the earth. Whether you need a simple one page website or a complex eCommerce site I can help.

Industry standards

I use industry standard tools & techniques to ensure your website isn't out of date.

A safe pair of hands

I've been building websites since the birth of the internet for several years. During that time I've used all the major web programming languages - PHP, ASP.NET, Java, JavaScript.

CMS

Nowadays I use an Open Source Content Management System (CMS) and good ole' HTML, CSS & JQuery. My current go-to CMS is CMS Made Simple but I have used WordPress and Drupal.

Support

Once your website is built I will be around to provide support. I provide a full support service so you can stop worrying about your website and get on with your job.

Website hosting

I can host your website, provide a monitoring service and ensure your site is protected against security breaches.

Staff training

I provided Staff training, documentation & support.

Updating content

If you prefer not to get involved in updating your website content I can do that too.

It doesn't matter how involved you want to be with your website, I can provide an appropriate level of support.

And if you have an existing website that you need looking after, I can do that too.
